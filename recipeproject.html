Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Milk,Ghee,Butter
  • Sugar,Baking powder,flour
  • Oil and nuts

Recipe

  1. Prepare the syrup and make the dough using milk powder, flour, and soda in a bowl
  2. Shape and fry the balls
  3. Soak in syrup
  4. Serve,eat and enjoy!!!! Tasty gulab jamuns are ready .

Barfi

Ingredients

  • Condensed milk,milk powder and ghee.
  • Cardamom powder.
  • Chopped nuts,oil and edible silver leaves.

Recipe

  1. Heat the mixture of ghee and milk stirring constantly
  2. Stir in cardamom powder and chopped nuts.
  3. Set barfi in a cool and greased plate and then garnish with chopped nuts and silver edible leaves.
  4. Cut it, serve and enjoy it!!!!.
